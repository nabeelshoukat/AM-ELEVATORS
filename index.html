      Hospital Lift

    An elevator is technically complex lifting device which should ensure maximum reliability and safety of operation. In hospitals and clinics, special attention is paid to these parameters, since any failure can lead to serious consequences. That is why lift equipment for hospitals should have additional protection, ensuring uninterrupted operation even in the absence of electricity. Thus, modern hospital elevators are equipped with batteries that allow continuing riding and supply with lighting sources.
